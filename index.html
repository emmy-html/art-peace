About Art Peace

Who We Are

The Art Peace Collective is a group of UIC students from various backgrounds, who are dedicated to spreading the message of healing through art.

What We're Doing

We are hosting workshops that aim to foster a safe space for people to create art across a variety of mediums in a safe environment.

When & Where You Can Find Us

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we will be hosting any and all workshops virtually for the forseeable future. This allows us to keep our work as accessible and as safe as possible.

Programming

Our first workshop, on Tuesday, December 1st, 2020, will be held over Zoom. This event is free, but registration is required. You will receive a confirmation email with the Zoom link upon completing your registration, and expect to receive follow up email(s) regarding planned programming and supply lists. Supply lists will be provided at least 48 hours before the event, can be found here, and will be updated as needed.
